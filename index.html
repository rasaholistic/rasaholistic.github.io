Rasa Holisitic

  • "Rasa" is the essential element of any work of visual, literary, or performing art that can only be suggested, not described. It is a kind of contemplative abstraction in which the inwardness of human feeling suffuses the surrounding world of embodied forms.
  • It is said that one can “taste dharma” – in that you know the truth when you taste it. Enlightenment has one flavor. This flavor is "Rasa!"
  • "Holistic" = whole person, body, mind, spirit. At Rasa Holistic, we utilize different modalities to encourage integration!
